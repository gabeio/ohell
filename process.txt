1. full deck of cards
2. shuffle deck of cards
2a. ask for amount of players
3. ask for amount of hands
3a. game loop until amount of hands left is zero
4. deal amount of hands as cards to everyone (assuming there is enough)
4a. subtract 1 card for every hand already played
5. ask for tricks
6. tricks can not add up to amount of cards in the hand
7. first play rotates
7a. a card off the deck is flipped to see the trump suit
8. first player can put down anything
9. next player must follow *suit* unless they do not have the suit or wish to trump (same for all other players)
10. point goes to highest trump suit card, then highest suit card
